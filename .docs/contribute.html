Contributing to Triva

Triva is an open-source project built for long-term stability, transparency, and enterprise-grade reliability. Contributions are welcome at all levels.

Ways to Contribute

  • Bug fixes and regressions
  • Performance improvements
  • Documentation enhancements
  • Test coverage and edge cases
  • Design and architecture feedback

Development Principles

Contributions should prioritize determinism, clarity, and minimal surface area. Triva avoids hidden state, background processes, and non-transparent behavior.

Sponsorship

If Triva is critical to your business or product, sponsorship helps fund long-term maintenance, security audits, and continued development.

Call to Action

Whether you’re submitting a pull request, improving docs, or sponsoring development, your involvement helps shape the future of Triva.
